{{ accomodation.title }} is located in Split, just a 5-minute walk from the UNESCO-protected Diocletian's Palace.
New and renovated apartments are decorated with a combination of luxury and traditional details. Air conditioning and Wi-Fi are available. Bačvice Beach is a 15-minute walk away.

All accommodation units feature a seating area with a flat-screen cable TV. Modern spaces and exposed stone walls create a luxurious and traditional atmosphere, a kitchenette and a bathroom equipped with contemporary amenities.

The nearest restaurant is a 5-minute walk away, and many other bars, shops, galleries and restaurants can be found in the Old Town of Split. Marmontova Street is full of shops and is only a few steps away.

There is a diving center in Bačvice, a playground is a 15-minute walk away. Marjan Forest Park is located nearby and offers many opportunities for hiking and picnics.

The Main Bus and Train Stations and Split Ferry Port, with daily lines to nearby islands, are only 700 meters away. Split Airport is less than 25 km from the resort. Free parking is available near the property.
